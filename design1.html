Sunrise light manipulation

This image has been meticulously edited using a slightly warm color tone to evoke a sense of comfort and natural elegance. The warmth subtly enhances skin tones and environmental hues, creating an inviting visual atmosphere without overpowering the original colors. Additionally, a controlled manipulation of sunlight has been applied—carefully introducing soft highlights and directional light flares to simulate the gentle presence of natural sunlight. This technique enriches the photograph’s depth and realism, adding a cinematic layer to the composition while maintaining the integrity of the original scene. The result is a refined visual narrative that feels both authentic and artistically enhanced, designed to engage the viewer emotionally while showcasing a harmonious balance between nature and creative editing.

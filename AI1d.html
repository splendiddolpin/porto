AI Photo Generation


This AI-generated image captures a nostalgic and cinematic atmosphere, depicting two individuals seated on the floor in front of a retro CRT television. They are both wearing matching black jackets with bold red text on the back reading "The Warriors," referencing the iconic 1979 cult film. The TV screen also displays the same title, reinforcing the theme. Each person is holding a classic game controller, suggesting they are playing a vintage video game adaptation of the movie. The lighting is warm and moody, evoking a 1980s or early 2000s aesthetic, enhanced by the vintage furniture and analog television. The subjects are turned slightly toward the camera, giving a sense of intrigue and connection with the viewer, while also remaining immersed in their shared gaming experience. The image artfully blends retro pop culture with modern digital artistry.

Discover the rich, authentic flavors of Gambuhan Coffee, a local coffee brand proudly rooted in the highlands of Gambuhan Village, Pulosari, Pemalang. Grown with care by dedicated farmers and processed using traditional methods, our coffee brings the essence of the village to every cup.This website is a portfolio project to support the promotion of this local small business (UMKM), highlighting its products, stories, and impact on the community. Through clean design and informative content, we aim to connect coffee lovers with the unique heritage and potential of Gambuhan Coffee.Join us in celebrating local craftsmanship and taste the difference of homegrown excellence.

  • Brainstorming

    • The project began with a direct interview with Mr. Suswanto, the local coffee farmer and key figure behind Gambuhan Coffee. This step was crucial to gather authentic information about the brand, including its origin, production process, values, and business challenges. The insights obtained helped shape a clear understanding of what the website should communicate and who the target audience is. This stage laid the foundation for the entire design direction and content structure of the website.

  • Prompt Building

    • Next came the prompt building phase, where a detailed and carefully crafted text prompt was written for the AI image generation model. This included specific visual elements such as “two people sitting on the floor wearing matching black jackets that say 'The Warriors' in red,” “playing retro video games,” “CRT television showing ‘The Warriors’ title screen,” and “warm, moody, cinematic lighting.” Attention was given to the clothing style, facial expressions, and composition to ensure the prompt conveyed the exact atmosphere and storytelling desired.

  • Image Generation

    • Once the prompt was finalized, the image generation was initiated using an AI tool capable of interpreting the descriptive prompt. Several images were generated, and the most visually accurate and emotionally resonant result was selected. This phase focused on refining visual coherence, lighting tone, and overall aesthetics that best represented the original concept.

  • Face Swap

    • Finally, a face swap technique was applied to improve facial accuracy and better match the intended likeness of the people in the image. This helped ensure realism and personal connection, particularly if the image was meant to resemble specific individuals. The final output reflects a seamless blend of AI creativity and human-guided control, resulting in a compelling and authentic visual narrative.
